This is the Figma prototype for Cook & Count. It showcases:
- Splash screen
- Create account/login
- Whistle timer setup and counter
- Notification and sound settings
- Recipe/chef mode
- Alerts for cooking completion

All screens are designed to guide first-time pressure cooker users.
